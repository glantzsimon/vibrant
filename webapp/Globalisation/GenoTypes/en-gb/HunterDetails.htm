Type 1 - The Proactive

Often taller than average, slender and intense, with an overabundance of adrenaline and a fierce, nervous energy that calms down with age.

Vulnerable to systemic burnout when overstressed and their modern challenge is to conserve energy for the long haul.

Slogan: shoot first, ask questins later!

Exercise

Type 1 people need regular, vigorous exercise to stay fit and healthy, reduce stress, and increase endurance. To accomplish this, about 40 minutes of exercise daily, or most days is ideal. It's important to warm up for at least 5–10 minutes with some gentle stretching before beginning any aerobic activity.
