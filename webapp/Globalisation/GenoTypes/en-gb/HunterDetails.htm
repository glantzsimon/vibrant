Exercise

Hunter GenoTypes need regular, vigorous exercise to stay fit and healthy, reduce stress, and increase endurance. To accomplish this, about 40 minutes of exercise daily, or most days is ideal. It's important to warm up for at least 5–10 minutes with some gentle stretching before beginning any aerobic activity.
