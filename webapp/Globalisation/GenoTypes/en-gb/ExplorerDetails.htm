The Explorer’s vulnerability to hormonal imbalances and brain strain can be overcome with a balanced diet and lifestyle.

  • Trunk length is usually longer than leg length, with longer upper legs than lower.
  • Explorers tend toward asymmetry, with different fingerprint patterns on each hand and reversed finger lengths for their gender.
  • Many left-handers are Explorers, as are individuals with Rh-negative blood type and "non-secretor" ABO blood types.

  • GT4 Explorers face challenges detoxifying drugs, carcinogens, and environmental pollutants.
