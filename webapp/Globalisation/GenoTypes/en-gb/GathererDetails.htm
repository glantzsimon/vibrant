Gatherers often have wonderful, warm, sensuous personalities. An affinity for home, hearth, people, and food makes them beloved friends and partners.

Intellectually adventurous and are often among the first to adapt to new ideas and methods. Although easygoing, they are highly principled and often tenacious defenders of justice.

Slogan: Whoever dies with the most wins

Gatherer Strengths

  • Phenomenal capacity for prolonged concentration
  • Natural problem solver
  • Early adopter of new, relvolutionary ideas
  • Sweet natured
  • Terrific mental endurance
  • Highly motivated
  • Fertile
  • Typically ages well
  • Good immunity

Gatherer Vulnerabilities

  • Prone to emotional highs and lows
  • Struggles with motivation to exercise
  • Struggles with appetite regulation
  • Weight gain
  • Estrogen dominance
  • Accumulation of toxins in tissues
  • Hypothyroidism
