Teachers are typically sinewy and flexible, with an amazing adaptability, the Teacher is a balance between opposing and often contradictory forces.

Blessed with a tolerant immune system, the Teacher can be burdened by excess altruism, leading to problems finding and dealing with the bad guys.

Slogan: why can't we all just get along?

Teacher Strengths

  • Natural exuberance
  • Calm and steady nature
  • Soul or an artist
  • Deep relationship with Nature
  • Meta-analytical
  • Very adaptable to new environments
  • Powerful spiritual energy
  • Ages gracefully
  • Tremendous mechanical strength and flexbility

Teacher Vulnerabilities

  • Sensitive digestive system
  • Immune system can be overly tolerant
  • Can become excessively detail-oriented

Exercise

Teacher GenoTypes need regular, vigorous exercise to keep their metabolism in balance, increase endurance, and maximize their resistance. You’ll need to blend both demanding and less demanding exercises for a total of 40 minutes, 4–5 times weekly. Be sure to warm up for at least 5–10 minutes with some gentle stretching before beginning any aerobic activity.

See the "Recommended Activities" section for more information.
