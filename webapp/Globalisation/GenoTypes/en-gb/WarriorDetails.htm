Warrior are long, lean, and healthy in youth, but typically subject to a bodily rebellion in midlife.

With the optimal diet and lifestyle, the Warrior can overcome the quick-aging metabolic genes and experience a second "silver age" of health.

Slogan: time flies when you're having fun

Warrior Strengths

  • Charismatic
  • Quick, nimble, computer-like mind
  • Great determination
  • Ox-like strength
  • Recovers well from illness
  • Often very beautiful in youth

Warrior Vulnerabilities

  • Can become irritable when stressed
  • Struggles to relax
  • Skin flushes easily
  • May be prone to excessive clotting
  • Thrify metabolism often sets in later in life
  • Ages early and steadily
  • Immunosuppression due to stress
  • Insulin resistance
  • Obesity
  • Chronic digestive problems
  • Cramps
  • Bloating
  • Hormone imbalances
