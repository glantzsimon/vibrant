Pitta Dosha

Pitta dosha is made up of fire and water and rules digestion and emotions. It is correlated with summer season, Pitta body types are usually medium build, athletic and muscular. THey often have oily and / or freckled skin and straight hair. Pitta people often have sharp intellects and are typically quite driven, making them confident and strong leaders.

Vulnerabilities

When pitta is imbalanced, people often experience physical symptoms such as heartburn, indigestion and even conditions like psoriasis, alopecia, etc. Emotionally, pitta types may become angry more easily than the other types, exhibiting impatience and competitiveness. They're also known to be more jealous when out of balance.

How to Balance Pitta Dosha

  • Eat foods that are nourishing and refreshing, with sweet, bitter, or astringent tastes. Mild herbs and spices can also be beneficial.
  • increase intake of leafy greens, watery and cool fruits and vegetables, salads.
  • Decrease intake of hot and pungent foods, spicy foods, sour fruits, meat, oils, dairy, eggs and sugar
  • Engage in consistent, non-competitive exercise such as hiking or swimming.
  • Spend more time in Nature.
  • Try to maintain a healthy work-life balance and engage in self-care
  • Avoid stimulants like coffee, acidic foods, and foods that are fermented, heavy, or particularly spicy.
