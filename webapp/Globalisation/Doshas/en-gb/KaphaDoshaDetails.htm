Kapha Dosha


kapha dosha is made up of the elements water and earth. It is a stabilising energy that keeps things together and provides support. Kapha dosha people experience life in a slow, intentional manner, rarely becoming upset and usually thinking before they act. Their demeanor is typically calm, caring and empathetic, patient, positive, and romantic. Physically, their stability is represented by a strong frame, healthy hair, and clear and smooth skin. Their facial features, like eyes and lips, are typically more pronounced than those of the other doshas. Kapha dosha is drawn to leisurely activity and enjoys routine in their life.


Vulnerabilities

Unbalanced kapha dosha may feel physically congested, such as in the sinuses and respiratory system. Their usual stamina may be interrupted by periods of sluggishness and depression and a noticeable withdrawal from their social circles.


How to Balance Kapha Dosha

  • Eat foods that are flavored with pungent spices and that are warm and nourishing. Lighter proteins are also recommended in favor of heavier ones. These options help stimulate the metabolism and combat the kapha’s sluggish nature.
  • Increase intake of leafy greens, pungent foods, light fruits and veggies, spices.
  • Decrease intake of starchy vegetables, oils, dairy, fatty fruits and nuts, fried and heavy foods
  • It's beneficial to occasionally shaking up your routine to incorporate more physical stimulation, such as exercise.
  • Find ways to maintain a warm body temperature, such as regular sauna use, or on a walk in the sunshine.
  • Try to wake up earlier and refrain from taking naps. It’s more beneficial to kapha dosha people to stay active and engaged during the waking hours.
