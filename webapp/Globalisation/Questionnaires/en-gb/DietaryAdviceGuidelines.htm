When considering the dietary advice contained in this section, it's important to remember that it’s really about what you eat, not what you don't eat. Focussing on consuming plenty of what nourishes you is what will help you to heal or maintain optimum balance. So try not to focus on all the things you need to avoid, and place your attention on the foods that are most beneficial to you.
