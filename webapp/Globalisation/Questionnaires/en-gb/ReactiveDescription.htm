A reactive strategy responds to the environment in more of an aggressive, proactive way. The body reacts swiftly to infection or allergens and, similarly, the character is often quick to respond to perceived threats or injustices. This worldview is inflammation-based. The level of reactivity This strategy provides for good immunity aginst infection and toxicity. However, the downside of this worldview is that, when out of balance, it comes at the expense of damaging the body’s own tissues as a by-product of all this reactivity, the type of friendly fire that leads to autoimmune disease. Thankfully, there are plenty of things that can be done to maintain balance.
