To perform the tests necessary for the Genetic Profile Calculator, you will need:

  • Thirty minutes
  • An assistant to help you measure
  • A firm measuring tape, the kind you can use to measure your height
  • A soft measuring tape, the kind you can use to waist and hips
  • A small ruler that you can use to measure your finger length in millimeters
  • A standard kitchen chair with backrest
  • Your ABO blood group
  • Fingerprint kit or "Fingerprint Imager" app

If you are not sure of your blood group, you can either:

  • Call your doctor's office
  • Buy a finger-prick blood test kit online
  • Donate blood (they will test your blood)
